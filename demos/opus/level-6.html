Design

Straight lines. One curve.
That curve is the color.

The exoskeleton refuses to bend, so we bent the light instead — a four-stage rose that shifts from chrome to void depending on where the sun is.

30X Exoskeleton

Cold-rolled stainless, 3mm thick. Dent-resistant, scratch-resistant, and now genuinely difficult to lose in a parking lot.

Tri-Motor Torque

845 hp across three independent motors. Torque vectoring at each corner, four-wheel steering under 40 mph.

Armor Glass

Ultra-hard borosilicate laminate. Rated for a Class 2 impact. We recommend not testing this on camera.

Vault Bed

6.5-foot bed with a powered tonneau, 120V and 240V outlets, and 67 cubic feet of lockable storage.
