Support

Asked before you had to

Is the deposit really refundable?

Yes — 100%, any time before your truck enters the coating studio. One click in your account, funds back in 3–5 business days. No calls, no retention scripts.

Does the pink finish void the factory warranty?

No. Magnolia Rosé is applied by a certified body-modification partner and carries its own 10-year finish warranty on top of the vehicle's standard coverage. Powertrain and battery warranties are untouched.

How durable is Magnolia Rosé, honestly?

It's a ceramic-bonded coating rated 9H hardness — the same rating as the panel it bonds to. Gravel roads, car washes, and ten years of UV are in the test protocol. Deep gouges can be spot-repaired at any partner studio; your serial file stores the exact batch tint.

When will my truck be delivered?

First deliveries begin Q2 2027, in serial-number order. Reserving today puts you in the low 300s; your configuration call locks a delivery window you'll see before paying anything further.

Only 500 — what happens when they're gone?

The run closes. Magnolia Rosé № 001 is retired with it — the formula is contractually limited to 500 vehicles, which is precisely why № 047 already trades above sticker.
